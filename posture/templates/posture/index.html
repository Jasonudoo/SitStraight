SitStraight is a free and open source application that helps you correct your posture when sitting at the computer

Start

How does it work?

SitStraight uses the webcam of your computer to determine if you are sitting upright or slouching. If you are slouching you will get an alert to correct your posture.

What about privacy?

Your images are never saved on the server. SitStraight only records the coordinates so your pictures are not accesible.

Health Benefits

It is estimated that 149 million days of work per year are lost because of low back pain. By correct your posture, you are improving your overall health.
